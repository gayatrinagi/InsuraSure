About InsuraSure

InsuraSure is a machine learning–powered web app that predicts health insurance premiums from a small set of inputs (age, sex, BMI, smoker, children, region). It goes beyond a static calculator with explainable AI and a built-in BMI calculator, helping you understand what your premium could be and why.

Who It’s For & Why It Matters

Individuals comparing plans, students learning ML for real-world finance/health, and anyone curious about how lifestyle affects insurance costs. By connecting health metrics → premium impact, InsuraSure helps you Predict. Protect. Prepare.
